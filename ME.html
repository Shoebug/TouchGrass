Amadiya, Iraq

Located in the north of Iraqi Kurdistan, only 10km away from the Turkish border, Amadiya is a lovely, very photogenic village located on the flat top of a mountain, which has no less than 5,000 years of history.

Shibam, Yemen

Listed as a UNESCO World Heritage site since 1982, Shibam is a 7000-inhabitant town, located in the middle of nowhere, composed of 9-story mud-brick buildings. Don't forget your life insurance!

Dubai, UAE

With skyscrapers where you don't see the top, Dubai is an ideal destination if you are middle-class and want to feel like the rich arab prince that you wish you were.

Aleppo, Syria

If you love the scent of post-conflict ambiance, Aleppo is perfect for keeping you on your toes for any pipebombs laying around.
