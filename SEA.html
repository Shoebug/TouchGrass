Tokyo, Japan

Immerse yourself in the vibrant heart of Japan by visiting its capital city, Tokyo. This bustling metropolis is a mix of ultra-modern skyscrapers and traditional temples. Explore the iconic districts of Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Akihabara, where you can find the latest technology, fashion, and entertainment. Don’t forget to visit the serene Meiji Shrine and the historic Asakusa district, home to the famous Sensoji Temple. Savor some of the world’s best sushi at the Tsukiji Fish Market or indulge in a bowl of steaming ramen at one of Tokyo’s many eateries.

Singapore

A well developed, technologically thriving city, Singapore is one of the famous places to visit in Southeast Asia. Be it a holiday with your family or a sweet getaway with your loved one, you can always count on this place for an exquisite experience. Isn’t it one of the best countries to visit in southeast Asia? Hands down, it is. The adventure activities in Singapore are galore.

Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur is another one of the happening southeast Asia destinations. The city which is also the capital of Malaysia, is a busy town. One of the top places to see in Southeast Asia, the capital is known for shopping and amazing culinary scenes. Enjoy a family vacation here and if like some adventure, then trek to the spectacular Batu Caves. There are plenty of day trips for couples in Kuala Lumpur for a serene getaway.

Perhentian Islands

The Perhentian islands are a small group of coral-fringed islands that are situated off the coast of northeastern Malaysia. The islands are an ideal destination for backpacker travelers as it offers cheaper accommodation. The number of things to do in Perhentian Islands will blow your mind away.
