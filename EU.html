Riga, Latvia

A City full of beautiful, modern sites. It is the most happiest City according to reliable statistics. The locals are welcoming and friendly.

Svalbard, Norway

Svalbard, an archipelago of nine islands between mainland Norway and the North Pole. If you’ve ever dreamt about visiting the Arctic, then a trip to Svalbard is the best place to go.

Utrecht, Netherlands

The city is over 2000 years old, the Romans built a fortress at what is now Dom Square, and buildings from the middle ages still stand in the city centre. It has all the charming Dutch canals of the capital.

Porto, Portugal

The ancient, picturesque, hilly city on the banks of the river Douro is full of architecture, great food, beautiful natural parks, and of course, a lot of Port. The hills are pretty steep, but it’s worth winding your way up to some of the less touristy neighbourhoods high above the river.
